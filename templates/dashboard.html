Welcome to Document Compliance Checker

Our system ensures your documents meet the required standards and provides actionable recommendations to improve compliance.

What We Do

Our website provides innovative solutions to help you ensure your documents adhere to your given templates. We combine cutting-edge technology with user-friendly design to deliver exceptional value to our customers.

Our Features

Compliance Checker:Our System uses advanced machine learning, template matching and optical character recognition technology to calculate how well your document complies with the template.

Machine learningOur system uses an advanced machine learning algorithm to calculate compliance scores for you. It assigns a weight of 30%,30%,40% to your document's heading, formatting and content similarity respectively

Optical Character RecognitionOur system uses OCR technology to "read" your document and compare it to the template. Do note that only docx and pdf files can be uploaded

How It Works

Our process is simple and straightforward:

  1. Sign up for an account
  2. Upload your template
  3. Upload your file
  4. Hit the Check compliance button
  5. Sit back and wait for our model to give you your compliance score.
